With this change
\begin{itemize}
  \item 1 app failed \textbf{rule 2.1} and \textbf{rule 2.2} 
  (Walkie Talkie)

  \item 1 app failed \textbf{rule 4}

  \item 8 apps failed \textbf{rule 4 and 5}
\end{itemize}
